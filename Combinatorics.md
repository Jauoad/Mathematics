# Combinatorics

## Factorial

$$
n! = n \times (n-1) \times (n-2) \times \cdots \times 1
$$

## Binomial Coefficient

$$
\binom{n}{k} = \frac{n!}{k!(n-k)!}
$$

## Permutations of n Objects Taken k at a Time

$$
P(n, k) = \frac{n!}{(n-k)!}
$$

## Combinations of n Objects Taken k at a Time

$$
C(n, k) = \frac{n!}{k!(n-k)!}
$$
