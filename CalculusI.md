# Calculus I

## Derivative of a Function

$$
f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x + h) - f(x)}{h}
$$

## Power Rule for Differentiation

$$
\frac{d}{dx} (x^n) = n \cdot x^{n - 1}
$$

## Product Rule

$$
(f \cdot g)' = f' \cdot g + f \cdot g'
$$

## Chain Rule

$$
\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{dy}{du} \cdot \frac{du}{dx}
$$
