# Calculus 2 (Integration)

## Definite Integral

$$
\int_a^b f(x) \, dx
$$

## Integration by Parts

$$
\int u \, dv = uv - \int v \, du
$$

## Fundamental Theorem of Calculus

$$
\int_a^b f'(x) \, dx = f(b) - f(a)
$$

## Trigonometric Integrals

$$
\int \sin(x) \, dx = -\cos(x) + C
$$

$$
\int \cos(x) \, dx = \sin(x) + C
$$
